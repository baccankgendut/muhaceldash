Game of Thrones' Richard Madden Slams Cara Delevingne as &quot;Ungrateful&quot;

Publish date: 2024-09-05

It’s safe to assume that Richard Madden is not a fan of Cara Delevingne’s. The Game of Thrones alum, 29, called the supermodel-turned-actress, 22, “ungrateful” following her now viral cringeworthy July interview with Good Day Sacramento.

"It was unprofessional,” the Scottish actor, who played King of the North Robb Stark, told Style magazine of Delevingne’s disastrous interview. "It made her seem ungrateful. She showed her age. For Cinderella, I did six weeks of those interviews, where you get asked the same eight questions."

Madden even suggested that fellow Brit Delevingne shouldn’t quit her day job as a model. “If you’re not capable of doing that gracefully, then don’t do it,” he continued.

Ed Norton’s Early Life and Career

Edward Norton was born in Boston, Massachusetts, and raised in Columbia, Maryland. Norton’s breakthrough came in 1996 when he starred in the film “Primal Fear,” earning him an Academy Award nomination for Best Supporting Actor.

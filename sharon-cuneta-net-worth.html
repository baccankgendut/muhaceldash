What is Sharon Cuneta's Net Worth?

Sharon Cuneta is a Filipino singer, actress, television host and reality show judge who has a net worth of $10 million. Sharon Cuneta was born in Pasay, Metro Manila, Philippines in January 1966. She has starred in more than 50 films and several TV series including The Sharon Cuneta Show, Sharon, The Biggest Loser: Pinoy Edition, and more. Cuneta has also recorded 40 albums starting when she was 12 years old in 1978. She earned a Lifetime Achievement Award by the Board of Directors of the Phil. Association of Recording Industry in 2002. In 2011 she signed a contract with TV5 for $21 million. Cuneta has won several awards including many time Box Office Queen as well as Ms. RP Movies, Outstanding Achievement by a Female Recording Artist, and Film Actress of the Year.  Sharon married Francis Panglinan in 1996 and was previously married to Gabby Concepcion.

Endorsements: Sharon Cuneta is considered one of the most valuable Filipino product endorsers. She has endorsed everything from McDonald's to Coca Cola to Globe Telecom.

Burning Man 2016 Fashion as Seen on Katy Perry, Karlie Kloss, More Celebs

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Celebrities rock outrageous styles for Burning Man every year and the 30th annual music and art festival in Nevada's Black Rock Desert was no exception. For 2016's event that kicked off August 28, Katy Perry, Cara Delevingne, Paris Hilton and Karlie Kloss went wild with multicolored braids, eye-popping manicures and futuristic getups worthy of Mad Max. Scroll through to see the stars' boldest outfits from the dust-filled, eco-friendly celebration in the middle of nowhere (about 20 miles from the nearest town).

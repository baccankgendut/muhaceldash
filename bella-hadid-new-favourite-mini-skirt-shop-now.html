Bella Hadid's new favourite mini skirt is under 30 shop now

Publish date: 2024-09-03

Attaining that much sought-after supermodel style is officially easier than ever thanks to Bella Hadid's latest fashion venture. The 26-year-old has been working on an exciting collaboration with online clothing retailer About You, involving a capsule collection of Bella-approved pieces that are utterly perfect for SS23.

The supermodel may have made her name on the runway, but she certainly keeps busy when she is not gracing the catwalk. Bella is the proud co-founder of non-alcoholic drinks brand Kin Euphorics, and news of her second collaboration collection with About You proves that she is keen to bring her unique sense of style to the masses.

In a post shared on Instagram with her 58.4m followers, Bella gave fans an insight into what goes on at a photoshoot. In the exclusive behind-the-scenes video, the supermodel posed in pieces from the new line and got her hair and makeup touched up on set by her glam squad.

A quick peruse at the collection shows that this season Bella still head over heels for her beloved Y2K aesthetic. Featuring low waistbands, sheer lace cami tops, slip skirts and a dash of florals, the pieces perfectly capture her signature 'model off-duty' vibe.

"I designed a collection for @aboutyou Get it in now on aboutyou.com," Bella told fans in the caption. "The essentials I had a little feeling you might need in your closet. Made by me, for you. Thank you to the @aboutyou team for helping me create our second collection together."

Now that spring is well underway and the weather is encouraging us to ditch the layers in favour of a glimpse of skin, all we can think about is shopping one of Bella's mini skirts. The current object of our lust is the 'Penelope' design, a wrap over, utility-feel piece which features a slim welt pocket and topstitch detailing on the thick, low-rise waistband.

 The skirt comes in two super wearable shades, a soft khaki and a classic black which currently retail at $32.90 (£26.41) and $34.90 (£28.02) respectively. 

Both colourways would look great teamed with either a tight-fitting graphic tee with diamanté lettering (very 2000s It-girl) or a plunging bodysuit for maximum drama.

Bella is our current favourite affordable fashion queen, and long may she reign.

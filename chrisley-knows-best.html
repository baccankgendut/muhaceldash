Chrisley Knows Best News - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-08-23

Network:USA Network

Reality series Chrisley Knows Best premiered on the USA Network in March 2014. The series revolves around Todd Chrisley, a multimillionaire real estate mogul, his wife, Julie, and their kids, Chase, Savannah and Grayson. Todd’s children from his first marriage to Teresa Terry — Lindsie and Kyle — also appeared on early seasons of the show.

In November 2022, Todd and Julie were each sentenced to multiple years in prison after being convicted on federal charges of bank fraud, tax evasion and conspiring to defraud the IRS. The 10th and final season of Chrisley Knows Best aired in February 2023, one month after the couple reported to prison.

Growing Up Chrisley, a spinoff focusing on Chase and Savannah, premiered in 2019 and ran for 4 seasons until November 2022.

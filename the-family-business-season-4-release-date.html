The Family Business Season 4 Release Date Status: Is It Officially Renewed for 2022?

Publish date: 2024-08-26

The Family Business is an American crime drama series that is adapted from one of the bestselling book series written by Carl Weber.  It was picked up by BET for production back in 2018 and revolves around the Duncans, a respectable family that has and runs an exotic car dealership in New York.

The first season of the series was aired on BET from November 13, 2018, to January 29, 2019, with a total of eight episodes. Later in late 2019, it was renewed for a second season which premiered on BET+ on July 2, 2020.

Season 2 of The Family Business aired in two parts with the second part premiering on August 13, 2020, and the second season consisted of a total of 12 episodes. The third season of the series aired on October 14, 2021, with a total of twelve episodes.

The series received mostly positive reviews from the audience as well as critics and has an IMDB rating of 6.2 out of 10. Now fans are eager to know about season 4 of The Family Business and here’s what we know about it so far.

The Family Business Season 4 Release Date

The series was renewed for a fourth season on January 28, 2022, but there are no details shared about the release date. However, considering the release pattern of the previous seasons, we can expect the new season to premiere by the end of this year.

The Family Business Season 4 Expected Cast

Though nothing has been uttered regarding the cast of the upcoming season of the series we can expect the return of almost all the cast from previous seasons here are the names:

  • Ernie Hudson to portray the character of L.C. Duncan
  • Armand Assante to portray the character of Sal Dash
  • Valerie Pettiford to portray the character of Charlotte Duncan
  • Darrin Henson to portray the character of Orlando Duncan
  • Yadi Rivera portrays the character of Consuela Zuniga
  • Emilio Rivera to portray the character of Alejandro Zuniga
  • Carlos Sotelo to portray the character of Miguel Zuniga
  • Miguel A. Núñez Jr. to portray the character of Harris Grant
  • Javicia Leslie to portray the character of Paris Duncan
  • Tami Roman to portray the character of London Duncan Grant

Apart from them, Brely Evans and Ben Stephens who were portraying the character of Sonya Duncan and Curtis Duncan respectively will become the regular cast of the series. We can also expect other changes to the cast of Season 4 but the announcement is still awaited.

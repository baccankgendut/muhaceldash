Jinger Duggar Expecting Again 6 Months After Welcoming Baby Felicity? Picture Sparks Rumors!

Publish date: 2024-08-15

Fans of Counting On are now wondering whether or not Jinger Duggar is expecting once again, only six months after she gave birth to her and Jeremy Vuolo’s firstborn! The speculations started from a picture that the husband posted.

The snap shows the reality TV star all cuddled up on a comfy armchair with a hot drink in hand and reading a book.

‘She loves cozy winter evenings with a good book and a cup of tea,’ the hubby wrote in the caption.

But what exactly caused people to be suspicious about her expecting another child?

Fans know very well that Jinger is a big fan of coffee since she always raves about how much she loves it on social media.

As a result, many followers were quite surprised to learn she was drinking tea instead when that pic was taken.

Here are a couple of the reactions: ‘Is she pregnant again cause she isn’t drinking coffee?’ / ‘Tea instead of Coffee??? Maybe she is pregnant?’

Besides, even her in-laws started suspecting she was pregnant with baby Felicity last year during an episode when she said no to a cup of coffee.

With that in mind, it’s no wonder fans put so much importance on his particular detail.

But there were some who thought people assuming only based on that is ridiculous.

‘People ask some stupid questions,’ one follower commented, as another also agreed: ‘Shocker…coffee isn’t a drink for all day and you can have tea without being pregnant. Don’t be rude. Jeez.’

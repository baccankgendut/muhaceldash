FITE Introduces AEW Plus Subscription For International Customers; AEW Dynamite To Stream Live On Se

Publish date: 2024-08-17

Tony Khan posted the following update, revealing fans in the UK will have the option to watch All Elite Wrestling live on FITE. Each episode will be available for $2.99 an episode, or fans can get a per month subscription for $4.99.

AEW Dynamite episodes will stream live in UK on @FiteTV for $4.99 month/subscription or $2.99/episode. We can’t broadcast live free to air in the UK due to different ad break schedules than in US. ITV + ITV4 will show free to air replays making our show available to most UK fans!

FITE has also confirmed fans will be able to sign up for AEW Plus for $4.99 a month (the price point which Khan also announced); that will give them live and replay access to shows, with more details to be announced soon.

Earlier today, ITV revealed plans for their AEW broadcast, which would see Dynamite airing on a four-day delay. As noted in Khan’s latest post, the delay was due to ad break schedules being different in the United States.

Career

Hogan started professional wrestling in 1977, where his debut match was with Brian Blair in CWF. Later he would find a wrestling partner Leslie and together they formed The Boulder Brothers and signed in the Continental Wrestling Association. Hogan won his first professional wrestling championship in 1979, the NWA Southeastern Heavyweight Championship.

Hogan enjoyed mainstream popularity in his professional wrestling career from 1983 until his departure from the sport. His signature maneuvers were to point the challenger while shaking his finger, atomic drop, big Boot, body slam, elbow drop, multiple punch variations, multiple suplex variations, shoulder block, and showing down.

His finishing moves were axe Bomber and Atomic Leg Drop. During his time as a wrestler, he was able to win WCW World Heavyweight Championship 6 times, WWE Tag Team Championship 1 time with Edge, The Royal Rumble in 1990, and 1991. Hogan’s contract with WWE was terminated in 2015 after his racist comments.

After departure, Hogan went into films where he made appearances in films such as No Holds Barred 1989, Suburban Commando 1991, The A-Team 1985, among others. He also had his own reality television show known as Hogan Knows Best from 2005. As a musician, Hogan has released two music CDs, s Hulk Rules and The Wrestling Boot Band.

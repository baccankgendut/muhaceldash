Salma Hayek Is Topless, Radiant for Allure Magazine: Photos

Publish date: 2024-08-19

Au naturel, baby! Salma Hayek covers the August 2015 issue of Allure magazine, looking lovely as always and giving us just a peek at her lingerie. But inside the issue, the Mexican beauty poses topless—and looks positively radiant doing so.

Modeling a glam face—lined eyes, lush lashes, and rose lips—and a romantic updo, Hayek, 48, can be seen looking into a mirror while holding her bare breasts. Meanwhile, her glowing skin looks nearly wrinkle-free.

The secret behind her ageless appearance? Her own Nuance Salma Hayek line. “The tension in the skin is beginning to give up,” the actress tells Allure. “I use my AM/PM serum. I swear it works. I’m not lying! But you have to do it every day.”

As for those glorious locks, while Hayek says she likes to keep them straight—especially if she’s wearing a busy ensemble—she does consider tips from her husband, Francois-Henri Pinault, and their daughter, Valentina. “François always tries to make me wear the hair curled,” she shared. “If it was up to him, it would always be down, and it would always be curly. He says I look the best like that.”

Meet Preston Hemphill, The White Officer, Who Tased Tyre Nichols

Preston Hemphill (born in July 1996) is the 26-year-old white police officer that participated in the killing of the innocent Tyre (29). On January 7, 2023, MPD cops stopped a young guy named Tyre Nichols at a traffic light, but things started going berserk even after the driver’s total cooperation. Preston and his colleagues pulled out their guns at Deandre without any particular reason and started beating him.

Somehow Tyre escaped, but while he was trying to get away, Hemphill tased him. The scared Nichols could not go far, and little after, law enforcer units captured him, and the torture started.

The publicly released video clip revealed that motorist was kicked and punched in the chest, making his condition so bad that even medical attention could not save him.

Of all the cops that are visible in the bodycam clip, five were fired, and their names were made public, but there were two more officers whose names were kept secret. Finally, on January 30, Sixth officer Hemphill got recognized as one of the two officers. A public notice was also released by the concerned department confirming that it was Preston who tased Nichols.

From Where is Preston Hemphill?

According to sources, Preston Hemphill (age 26) was born in July 1996 in Tennessee in a catholic family. Sources also revealed that he belongs to an affluent family, although we do not know the names of his father and mother. Hemphill spent his entire childhood in Memphis and completed his education.

After graduation, he did a few jobs for a while and joined Memphis Police Force. For approximately 3 years, he served as a Police Officer in Tennessee, and nobody knew how sick his behavior was toward others.

When Tyre ran for his life, Preston could be heard saying, “I hope they stomp his ass,” which clearly confirms that he was not professional while doing his job.

Preston Hemphill Hiding His Identity

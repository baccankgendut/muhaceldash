Birth Name: Nikolla Christopher Shkreli

Place of Birth: Detroit, Michigan, U.S.

Date of Birth: July 30, 1981

Ethnicity: Albanian

Nickola Shreli is an American actor, producer, and writer. He has appeared in the films Abduction, Hostel: Part III, Low Winter Sun, and Cash Only, the latter of which he also wrote.

Nickola’s parents are from Albania. He has two children.

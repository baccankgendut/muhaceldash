What is Camille Guaty's Net Worth?

Camille Guaty is an American actress who has a net worth of $3 million. Camille Guaty was born in California in June 1976. From 2001 to 2002 she starred as Olivia in the television series Raising Dad. Guaty starred as Maria, the Maid on the TV series The Help in 2004. From 2006 to 2007 she starred as Franny Rios on the series The Nine and from 2005 to 2007 she had a recurring role as Maricruz Delgado on the television series Prison Break.

Guaty starred as Piper Nielsen on the TV series Las Vegas from 2007 to 2008. In 2009 she starred as Lita in the series Cupid. Guaty starred as Elena Velez in the series Happyland in 2014 and as Megan O'Brien in the series Scorpion from 2014 to 2015. In 2017 she starred as Nina in the television series Daytime Divas.

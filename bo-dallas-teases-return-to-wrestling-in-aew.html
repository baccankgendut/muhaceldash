Bo Dallas Teases Return To Wrestling In AEW

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Bo Dallas walked away from the world of pro wrestling after being released by WWE. Dallas was a talented wrestler with a great family pedigree. Bo is the brother of Bray Wyatt and the son of Mike Rotunda, better known to WWE fans as IRS. Fans have been wondering if we would ever see Bo step inside the ring again.

Mike Rotunda recently said he doesn’t think Bo Dallas and Bray Wyatt are finished. Bo was perfectly happy to live on farm and create content for YouTube. Now, he has teased that he may be making a return soon.

Bo Dallas recently appeared at the “For the Love of Wrestling” event where he was asked about a possible run in All Elite Wrestling. Dallas said he does plan to wrestle again within the next three months. He has his sights set on one of his greatest opponents from NXT.

Bo said he would love to work with PAC again. As Adrian Neville, PAC defeated Bo Dallas for the NXT Championship in 2014. The two kickstarted the hottest period in the promotional brand’s history.

Dallas never got to shine during his time in WWE after moving on from developmental. AEW has plenty of fresh matchups and a completely different perspective on booking. It could be the perfect place for a comeback.

Time will tell if AEW has any interest in Bo Dallas. The roster is already stacked from top to bottom. With Ring of Honor working on a TV deal, that load may be getting lighter. Bo Dallas would love to be along for the ride.

Natives Release New Single 'Warpaint'

Publish date: 2024-08-23

Natives might not be a name you know yet, but trust us soon that will change. Currently, out on tour with pop legends Busted, the quartet with their funky, synth-infused sound have been making waves over the last 12 months.

Just before heading out on their biggest tour to date, they’ve released their new single “Warpaint” and it’s a jam, to say the least. In this dull, dreary weather we love the catchy, tribal edge to this track with chants which you’ll be singing along to before the track is out. Natives have shown they’re on a clear, storytelling path here singing of overcoming something negative in your life and fighting to save you and those around you. Things in life won’t always be easy but Natives are spreading a message of hope and support to overcome it, with an infectious tune to accompany this message.

Like the sound of this track? Be sure to check out previous singles “Passion”, “Stop The Rain” and “Chasing Lions” taken from the band’s second album due for release in May. If you’re heading out to a Busted date then get learning the words! Natives are definitely one to watch in 2017, trust us on this.

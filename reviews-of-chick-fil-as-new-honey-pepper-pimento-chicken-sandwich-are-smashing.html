Reviews of Chick-fil-A's new Honey Pepper Pimento Chicken Sandwich are 'smashing'

Publish date: 2024-08-20

Chick-fil-A has just released its first new burger in nine years, the Honey Pepper Pimento Chicken Sandwich, and the reviews are incredible. Here’s what people are saying.

It’s a brand new take on the fast food chain’s Chicken Sandwich with never-used-before ingredients that put a flavorful spin on the familiar favorite.

Chick-Fil-A releases new Chicken Sandwich

The new sandwich features an original Chick-fil-A filet drizzled with honey, topped with pimento cheese and served on a warm, toasted bun with mild pickled jalapeños.

Pimento is a spread that’s made of cheese, mayonnaise and pimientos, also known as peppers or capsicums. It’s usually served on crackers and vegetables or in sandwiches.

A thick layer of the dip, which is a favorite in the Southern United States, is spread across the battered chicken filet to create a messy flavor explosion.

Chick-fil-A’s pimento is made with smoky green chili peppers and sweet roasted pimento peppers, blended with cheeses and seasonings.

The original Chicken Sandwich’s dill pickle chips are replaced with mildly spiced jalapeños and the whole thing is topped with honey for a sweet finish.

Honey Pepper Pimento Chicken Sandwich reviews

The sandwich was released on Monday (August 28) and many rushed to their local store to taste the new burger – and it didn’t disappoint.

Most people really loved it, with one person on Twitter calling it “certified gas” and others saying it’s “pretty stellar,” “smashing” and “elite”.

“Well, I tried it and its probably the best chicken sandwich I’ve ever eaten. Highly recommend,” someone else exclaimed.

“Not the best pimento cheese I’ve ever had by a long shot, but the flavor combination is on point,” another person added.

There were a few negatives, with some people arguing theirs didn’t have enough pimento and others saying it was too messy and sloppy.

However, it looks like Chick-fil-A has mostly smashed it with this one – so head to your nearest one to give it a try!

Be quick because it’s limited edition

Chick-fil-A hasn’t revealed an end date, but they have said the new sandwich is limited-edition, so it won’t be around forever.

Head to your closest restaurant sooner rather than later to make sure you don’t miss out – and be sure to wash it down with another exclusive item.

The scrumptious Caramel Crumble Milkshake also launched this week and is the perfect addition to the seasonal menu.

It’s made with creamy butterscotch caramel flavors, hand-spun Chick-fil-A Icedream and blondie crumbles all topped with whipped cream and a cherry.

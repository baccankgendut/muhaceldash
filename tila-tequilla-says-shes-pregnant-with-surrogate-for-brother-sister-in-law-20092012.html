TIla Tequilla Says She's Pregnant with Surrogate for Her Brother!

Publish date: 2024-08-22

More outrageous news from Tila Tequila: she's pregnant!

After proclaiming her engagement to heiress Casey Johnson Dec. 9, the MTV reality star tweeted her latest "big announcement" Sunday:

"I am going to become a SURROGATE MOTHER for my brother and his Wife!!…That is my xmas present to them," she writes.  "I'm pregnant!!!"

Earlier, she teased that she was giving her brother a present that would "CHANGE HIS LIFE AND MINE FOREVER!!!! ITS HUGE!"

Tila (real name: Tila Ngyuen), 27 has not yet provided additional details about the due date of the child or how she reached the momentous decision.

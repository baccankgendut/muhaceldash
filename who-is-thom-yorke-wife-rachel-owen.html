Who is Thom Yorke wife Rachel Owen?

Publish date: 2024-08-31

Thomas Edward Yorke, an English musician best known as the rock band Radiohead’s main singer and composer, was born on October 7, 1968.

He is a multi-instrumentalist who primarily performs on guitar and keyboards and is recognized for his falsetto. According to Rolling Stone, one of the greatest vocalists in history as well as one of the most important individuals of his generation.

Who is Thom Yorke’s wife Rachel Owen?

Thom Yorke of Radiohead and actress Dajana Roncione are reportedly married, according to Italian publications la Repubblica and Vanity Fair.

La Repubblica reports that the occasion took place in Sicily and that attendees adhered to coronavirus precautions. Greg Williams captured the images you see here.

According to Italian Vanity Fair, Yorke and Roncione began dating in 2017. The actress collaborated with Yorke on his ANIMA short film later in 2019.

Dr. Rachel Owen was Thom Yorke’s previous spouse. After having a son and a daughter together, they got a divorce amicably in 2015. Owen died in December of last year.
